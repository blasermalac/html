About Mountain Bike Club

We are a small company devoted to the highest quality mountain bikes and accessories. In our quest for quality, we have also collected information on our favorite local trails, for your convenience.

The company was started by Malac Blaser and Ryan Dille. Though it may have originally been created for the sole sake of passing a class, it soon became something more.
Since the fateful day that assignment was given, the company has staked its niche in Iowa biking. We have numerous locations around Iowa. Despite our rapid
growth, we are still dedicated to the small town feel that has come to be expected of Mountain Bike Club.
